3. Information We Collect

The App is designed to prioritize your privacy by limiting data collection to only what is necessary for its core functionality. Below is a detailed breakdown of the information we handle:

  • Local Usage Data: The App stores counters and labels you create (e.g., names or numbers for tracking purposes). This data is saved locally on your device using React Native’s AsyncStorage, a secure, device-specific storage mechanism. No data is transmitted to external servers or cloud services.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect or process any personally identifiable information, including but not limited to your name, email address, phone number, contact lists, location, or payment details.
  • Device Information: The App does not access or collect device-specific information, such as unique device identifiers (e.g., IMEI, UDID), IP addresses, operating system details, or hardware specifications.
  • No Third-Party Analytics or Tracking: We do not integrate or use third-party analytics, advertising, marketing, or tracking services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase). The App operates independently of external data collection tools.

4. Legal Basis for Data Processing

Since the App does not collect, store, or process personal data as defined by global privacy regulations (e.g., General Data Protection Regulation [GDPR], California Consumer Privacy Act [CCPA]), we do not rely on legal bases such as consent, legitimate interest, or contractual necessity. The minimal, non-personal data stored locally is solely for the App’s functionality and remains under your control.

5. Purpose of Data Processing

The sole purpose of storing data in the App is to enable its core functionality, such as creating, managing, and displaying counters or labels. This data:

  • Is used exclusively to provide the App’s features to you.
  • Is not shared, sold, or disclosed to any third parties.
  • Is not used for profiling, marketing, or any purpose beyond the App’s intended use.

6. Data Retention

All data created within the App (e.g., counters and labels) is stored locally on your device and remains there until you take action to remove it. Specifically:

  • Data persists until you manually delete it within the App or uninstall the App, which clears all associated data.
  • We do not maintain backups, copies, or archives of your data on external servers or databases.
  • If you reinstall the App, previously stored data may not be recoverable unless preserved by your device’s backup system (e.g., iCloud, Google Backup), which is outside our control.

7. User Rights

Since the App does not process personal data, many traditional data subject rights do not apply. However, we provide full transparency and control over the data you create within the App:

  • Access & Portability: Not applicable, as no personal data is stored. You can view all counters and labels directly within the App’s interface.
  • Rectification & Erasure: You can edit or delete any counters or labels at any time using the App’s built-in features. Uninstalling the App removes all data.
  • Restriction & Objection: Not applicable, as no data is processed or shared externally.
  • Withdraw Consent: Not applicable, as no consent is required for local, non-personal data storage.
  • Complaints: If you have concerns about our data practices, you may contact us (see Section 13) or, if applicable, lodge a complaint with a data protection authority in your jurisdiction.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the data stored locally on your device:

  • Storage Security: Data is stored using React Native’s AsyncStorage, which leverages secure, device-native storage mechanisms.
  • No Network Transmission: Since the App operates offline, there is no risk of data interception during transmission.
  • Device Responsibility: You are responsible for securing your device (e.g., using a PIN, password, biometric lock, or encryption) to prevent unauthorized access to App data. We cannot protect against physical access to an unlocked device.

While we implement standard security practices, no method of storage is completely secure. We encourage you to maintain up-to-date device security software and settings.

9. Third-Party Services

The App is entirely self-contained and does not connect to external servers, APIs, or third-party services. All operations, including data storage and processing, occur locally on your device. We do not embed or rely on third-party libraries or frameworks that collect data.

10. International Users and Data Transfers

Since the App does not transmit or store data externally, there are no cross-border data transfers. The App’s offline nature ensures compliance with international privacy laws without the need for data transfer agreements or mechanisms.
